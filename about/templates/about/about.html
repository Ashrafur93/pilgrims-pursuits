About Pilgrim's Pursuits

This website was developed by Ashrafur Ahmed as part of the Individual Capstone Project for the AI Augmented Full Stack Web Developer course by Code Institute. As such, it should not be relied upon for making informed decisions and may not be updated in the future.

Welcome to Pilgrim's Pursuits, your comprehensive guide to the sacred journey of Hajj. Our mission is to provide valuable insights, practical tips, and heartfelt reflections to help pilgrims navigate their pilgrimage with ease and confidence. From in-depth articles on historical sites and rituals to personal stories and expert advice, we aim to enrich your spiritual experience and connect you with a community of fellow pilgrims. Join us as we explore the profound significance of Hajj and support each other on this transformative journey of faith and devotion.
